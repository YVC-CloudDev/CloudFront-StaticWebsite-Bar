Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need prior experience with AWS?

Not at all. Our beginner courses start from the very basics of S3. You just need a free AWS account to get started.

Is CloudFront expensive?

AWS offers a generous free tier: 1 TB of data transfer and 10,000,000 HTTP/HTTPS requests per month for the first year. After that, you only pay for what you use.

Why use CloudFront instead of just S3 hosting?

CloudFront adds HTTPS, global CDN delivery, DDoS protection, and edge caching. It's more secure, faster, and often cheaper due to reduced S3 requests.

How do I get started with S3 + CloudFront?

Sign up for AWS, create an S3 bucket, upload your site, and create a CloudFront distribution pointing to your bucket. Our beginner course walks you through it step by step!
